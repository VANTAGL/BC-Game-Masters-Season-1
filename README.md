# BC Game Masters Season 1

![Dates](https://img.shields.io/badge/Date-Mar%202%20%E2%80%93%20Mar%2015%2C%202026-blue) ![Prize Pool](https://img.shields.io/badge/Prize%20Pool-$25,000-brightgreen)

**BC Game** and **@vantagl**, announce **BC Game Masters Season 1** — a Tier 2 Counter-Strike 2 online tournament with a **$25,000** prize pool, taking place between **March 2 - March 15, 2026**.

---

## Tournament Format & Schedule

### Stage 1 — Group Stage (Swiss, Best-of-3)
- **Dates:** March 2 — March 10, 2026
- **Teams:** 16 invited teams
- **Advancement:** 8 teams advance to the Main Event
- **Format:** Swiss / Best-of-3

### Stage 2 — Main Event (Single-Elimination, Best-of-3)
- **Dates:** March 12 — March 15, 2026
- **Teams:** 8 invited teams join 8 qualified teams (total 16)
- **Format:** Single-elimination / Best-of-3

---

## Invitations & Seeding
- **Total invitations issued:** 24 (issued 49 days prior — **February 3, 2026**)
- **Basis:** Official **February 2026 VRS** ranking
- **Breakdown:** Top 8 teams invited directly to the Main Event; 16 teams invited to the Group Stage
- **Invitations start at VRS rank:** **13**
- **If an invited team declines:** The invitation will be passed to the next eligible team in the VRS ranking

---

## Rules & Eligibility
- Teams must retain **a minimum of three players** from the invite-date roster to remain eligible for a direct VRS invitation.
- Invitations will be **withdrawn** if players are lost due to **Valve sanctions** or confirmed **cheating bans** issued by recognized anti-cheat providers (including **Akros** and **FACEIT**).
- **Open Qualifier:** No Open Qualifier for this edition
- **Tournament stages:** All stages are **online**
- **Rulebook:** [`BC Game Masters Season 1 Rulebook`](./BC-Game-Masters-Season-1-Rulebook.pdf)

---
